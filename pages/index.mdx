## Who this is for

<Callout type="info">
No coding experience required for most of this cookbook. Concepts and Recipes are written for anyone. The Tools section goes deeper for those who want to build.
</Callout>

This cookbook is for **three types of people**:

- **The curious newcomer** — you've heard about AI and want to actually understand it, not just the hype
- **The business operator** — you want to save your team hours every week by putting agents to work
- **The builder** — you're ready to ship something and need to pick the right stack

---

## A note on how to read this

Think of an AI agent like a **new team member** who never sleeps, never forgets, and can work across dozens of tools at once — but needs clear instructions. This cookbook teaches you how to give those instructions well, and which tools to put in their hands.

Let's start cooking. 👇
