# Recipe: Sales Prospecting

**Department:** Sales / Business Development
**Difficulty:** Beginner
**Time to set up:** 30–60 minutes

<ConceptVideo id="recipe-sales" title="Automating sales research and outreach" />

## The problem

The average sales rep spends 2+ hours every morning on prospecting prep — Googling accounts, reading LinkedIn profiles, checking for news, trying to personalize emails that don't sound generic. By the time they're done researching, their energy is gone.

## The agent's job

<Steps>

### Define the target profile
You tell the agent: target company size, industry, job title, geography, and any trigger events (e.g., recently raised funding, hiring for specific roles, expanding to new markets).

### Find prospects
The agent scans LinkedIn, company websites, news sources, job boards, and intent data platforms to find contacts that match your profile.

### Enrich the CRM
For each prospect found, it updates your CRM with current contact details, role, company info, and recent relevant activity — no manual data entry.

### Research each prospect
It pulls recent news about the company, the prospect's recent posts or activity, their company's job openings (which signal priorities), and any mutual connections.

### Draft personalized outreach
Using all the gathered context, it writes a first draft of an outreach email that references something specific — not a template. *"I saw your team just hired a VP of Operations and you're expanding into APAC — we help companies in exactly this transition..."*

### Sequence follow-ups
Based on opens, clicks, and replies, it schedules and sends follow-ups automatically. No manual tracking.

### Update CRM after every interaction
Every email sent, every reply received, every conversation logged — without the rep typing anything.

</Steps>

## Tools needed

- A CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
- An email platform (Gmail, Outlook)
- A web search tool
- An AI agent with access to the above

## Expected results

| Metric | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Prospecting prep time | 2 hours/day | 20 minutes/day |
| Cold outreach reply rate | 2–5% | 8–15% |
| CRM data accuracy | Inconsistent | Continuously updated |
| Pipeline generated | Baseline | +10–25% |

<Callout type="info">
**Real-world result:** One enterprise sales team reduced outreach prep from 2 hours to 20 minutes per rep, saving 5 hours per week. Cold reply rates increased from 3% to 10% through personalization.
</Callout>

## Getting started

1. **Connect your CRM** — most CRMs have native AI integrations or APIs. This is the most important step.
2. **Define your ICP clearly** — "mid-size tech companies" is not specific enough. "SaaS companies, 50–500 employees, VP of Finance, recently raised Series B" is actionable.
3. **Start with existing pipeline** — use the agent to enrich and research contacts you're *already* working with before turning it loose on net-new prospecting.
4. **Review the first 20 drafts** — spend time early correcting the agent's tone and style. It will calibrate to your voice quickly.
